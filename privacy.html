Privacy Policy

Official Privacy Policy

At SheedBox, we value your privacy. We only collect essential account details (like your email address and username) to provide and personalize our streaming services. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. By using our services, you agree to this policy. For any questions, contact us at info@sheedbox.com.

1. Overview

SheedBox operates a legal, premium digital streaming platform delivering video-on-demand content (movies and series) and live TV broadcasts. This Privacy Policy details how we handle user data and information safety in our operations.

2. Data Collection

We restrict data collection to the minimum details required to deliver the core service:

  • Account Profiles: Your email address and self-selected username collected during registration.
  • Activity Logs: Saved watchlist history and playback progress states, allowing you to resume viewing sessions.
  • Support Details: Inquiries, feedback, and support emails directed to info@sheedbox.com.

3. Processing Rationale

We process collected profile information to support core operations, specifically:

  • Providing registration, profile maintenance, and password settings.
  • Buffering streams, loading live TV directories, and storing bookmarks.
  • Analyzing aggregated viewing habits to improve performance.

4. Data Security

All passwords and communication profiles are encrypted using standard TLS protocols. We do not share, sell, or rent account information to external marketing agencies or brokers.

5. Embedded Third-Party Players

Certain live broadcasts or streams inside the platform are embedded directly from official creator feeds. When viewing these embedded streams, network traffic and viewing metrics connect to the respective third-party domain according to their own privacy guidelines. This ensures that visual views, traffic, and ad revenues continue to credit the original content creators.

6. Cookies and Preferences

SheedBox uses local functional cookies to keep sessions active, remember volume controls, and store selected interface localizations (English or Persian).

7. Rights & Contacts

You can request profile deletion or access your information anytime by mailing us at info@sheedbox.com
